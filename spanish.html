Practice Areas

As a sole practitioner, I have successfully represented clients in many legal areas. My main areas of practice involve contracts, small businesses, accidents & injuries, and landlords. Don’t hesitate to read more about the practice areas below or contact the attorney directly by filling out the free consultation form to your right.

Employment Law

If you are an employee, then you have rights that are protected under State and federal laws. I have defended clients in many employment law cases including: Discrimination, Wrongful termination, Breach of contract. Read more..

Contracts

If a contract has gone wrong or you are being sued over a contract, hiring an attorney will send out a strong message that you will fight for your rights. I have defended and protected clients against such issues, making sure to work together and communicate effectively. Read more..

Small Businesses
